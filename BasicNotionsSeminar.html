Basic Notions Seminar:

The Centre is running a hybrid basic notions seminar, aimed mainly towards PhD students, advanced master students, and early PostDocs. Anyone else who's interested is also welcome join and also to give talks if they like, however preference will be given to the mentioned target group. The main idea of the basic notions seminar is to bring together scientists from different backgrounds, who then provide low level introductory presentations of the basic concepts they use on a daily basis. This way, we improve our foundation for interdisciplinary work. on Mondays, 10:15 am via Zoom and at IMPAN's room 1. The Meeting-ID is 390 769 7241. Please contact Julian Brüggemann to obtain the passcode.

If you would like to give a talk as part of our Basic Notions Seminar please contact Julian Brüggemann.
