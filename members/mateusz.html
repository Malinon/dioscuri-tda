Mateusz Masłowski

I am a PhD student at the University of Gdańsk and the Dioscuri Centre in Topological Data Analysis, IMPAN. My research focuses on identifying topological invariants to detect phase transitions in discrete data sets, with potential applications in fields such as social networks and financial markets. I apply methods from topological data analysis and mathematical statistics to explore complex systems.
