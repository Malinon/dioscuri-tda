Bartosz Naskręcki

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Dioscuri Centre in TDA. I work at the interface of algebra, number theory and computer algebra. I completed PhD in Mathematics at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science of Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań. I worked in years 2014-2017 at the University of Bayreuth, Germany and University of Bristol,Bristol. During these post-docs I collaborated in the projects related to computational algebra and number theory. Since 2014 I am employed as an assistant professor at Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań.

In TDA team I work on a project in which I study topological and geometrical properties of crystals. I also try to extrapolate various algebraic techniques in the industrial and applied setting.
